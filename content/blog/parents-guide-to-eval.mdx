**Esports is more than "just playing games."** Many colleges across the U.S. now offer scholarships for competitive gamers—just like traditional sports. But unlike basketball or soccer, esports recruiting doesn't happen at a field or through a coach's referral—it happens online.

That's where EVAL comes in.

### EVAL gives students a platform to:

- **Showcase their gameplay and achievements**
- **Get evaluated through real performance data**
- **Connect with college recruiters and apply for scholarships**
- **Participate in organized tournaments and combines**

As a parent, this gives you a clear window into your child's esports experience—offering structure, accountability, and access to opportunity.

## Playing in Combines and Connecting with Colleges

EVAL hosts regular **Combines**—structured, team-based competitions where players are evaluated by recruiters. Think of them like esports scouting events.

### Students gain:

- **A ranking on the EVAL platform**
- **Opportunities to earn badges, combine invites, and even scholarship offers**
- **Experience playing in organized, competitive environments**

After combines, players can connect directly with college recruiters via the platform's messaging tools or by visiting school pages. Many colleges in our system actively respond to student inquiries, and EVAL makes it easy to have those conversations in a structured, professional way.
